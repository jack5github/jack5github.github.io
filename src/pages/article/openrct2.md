OpenRCT2 is an open-source mod for [RollerCoaster Tycoon 2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RollerCoaster_Tycoon_2) that adds support for plugins (such as the [Archipelago](/article/archipelago) randomiser), custom scenarios and cross-platform port-forwarded multiplayer.

## Installation

1. Own the [Steam version of RollerCoaster Tycoon 2](https://store.steampowered.com/app/285330/RollerCoaster_Tycoon_2_Triple_Thrill_Pack/) or a physical copy.
2. If using Windows, download and run the latest OpenRCT2 installer from the [official website](https://openrct2.io/). If using Linux, download the game from [Flathub](https://flathub.org/en/apps/io.openrct2.OpenRCT2).
3. To play online without port forwarding, follow the instructions for [ZeroTier](/article/zerotier).

## Tips for Making Bank

- **Pay off your loan**
  - Click on the coins button in the top-right and pay off as much of the loan as you can.
- **Plan roller coasters in advance**
  - Roller coasters suck up the most money when being constructed, especially if you make mistakes. Stick to a planned path and avoid having the coaster track loop back on itself.
- **Listen to guests' thoughts**
  - Click on the crowd button in the top-right. If any guest is saying something is really good value, click on the thought and increase the attraction's price by 10 cents. If they're saying something is too expensive, decrease it instead.
- **Hire only needed staff**
  - Click on the handyman button in the top-right to see the staff lists. It is most sensible to hire staff in groups of 2 handymen and 1 mechanic. Do not hire other types of staff. If the path becomes too big, use the blue footprints button to mark out a specific area each handyman should patrol in.
- **Max price umbrellas when raining**
  - When it is raining, click on an Information Kiosk and set the price of umbrellas to the maximum of $20. Remember to revert the price once it has stopped raining.
- **Delete pre-built paths**
  - Click on the path button in the top-right to open the Footpaths tool, select the *Construct bridge or tunnel footpath* option and then click on a tile with a path. Now press the demolish button repeatedly to earn $10 for each connected path.
- **Raise the entrance fee**
  - Click on the park entrance button in the top-right, select the coin tab and increase the admission price by $1 every so often. If a notification appears saying guests are no longer showing up to the park due to the high entrance fee, decrease it until they do.
- **Reduce research funding**
  - Click on the coins button in the top-right, select the beaker tab and choose *Minimal funding*. If this is still not giving you enough money, choose *No funding*.

## Known Bugs

- Clicking the coin tab of an individual parkgoer may crash the game.
