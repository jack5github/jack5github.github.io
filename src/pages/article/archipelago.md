# Archipelago

[Archipelago](https://archipelago.gg/) is a Cross-Game Modification System designed to combine and randomize multiple games together to create one huge multi-world. All items from every world are placed into an itempool, these items are then scattered to other games  Every game must work together to beat eachothers goals. 

For example if someone is playing Ocarina of Time they find a Item/Check for another game let's use Ratchet and Clank 3 as an example; They find an Infobot for Planet Aridia. Then if Ratchet and Clank 3's player finds an Item/Check for Ocarina of Time such as the bomb bag it gets sent to them.

## How does Archipelago work?

Archipelago uses `.apworld` files for every game outside of the website. (Stable games on the website are built into the Archipelago program) You can find these `.apworld`s on their [Discord](https://discord.gg/8Z65BR2) server via the future-game-design post channel or just exploring [Github](https://github.com/).

## How does Archipelago generate Multi-Worlds?

On the Archipelago program, you can generate the world manually; provided you have the YAML Files for each player inside of the world folder. However if you have games that are stable on the website, going to [Generate](https://archipelago.gg/generate) on the Archipelago website allows you to use a more simplier world generator. Upload your YAML files and the website will generate for you.

## I have generated the world. What do I do now?

You can do one of two things: Host it locally from the Archipelago program and play with it yourself or head over to their website and [host](https://archipelago.gg/uploads) there. When you host on the Archipelago website this allows you to play with other players from around the globe. Simply sharing the Room code you can now play with other players.

For example, an Australian player has hosted a game; An American can now play from across the world in the same room! How neat is that?
