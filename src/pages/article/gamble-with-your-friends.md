[Gamble With Your Friends](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3892270/Gamble_With_Your_Friends/) is a casino roguelike friendslop game with online multiplayer for up to 6 players. Everyone must work together to pay off their debt to the loan shark, which increases each day, all within 5 minutes of the casino's operating hours. You may make use of items to more easily make money from the casino.

2. Get into the limousine and drive to the casino.
3. Go to Floor 1. Have the Irishman set up at a Craps table preparing to bet the maximum, and have all other players stand over the table with baseball bats.
    - If you do not have a baseball bat, you can find one mounted on the wall in case of emergencies.
    - If there is no Craps table, you are screwed.
4. Allow the Irishman to play Craps for the whole 5 minutes. If the dice land on a number that would otherwise result in a loss, hit them with a baseball bat so they do not.

    - On all subsequent rolls, the number 7 is considered a loss. Avoid ⚀⚅, ⚁⚄ and ⚂⚃.
5. **Profit.** Repeat ad nauseam.
