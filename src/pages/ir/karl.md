Karl (real name *Leon Dreyfus*) is **Major Hawk10**'s player character in the **interp'd Reality** campaign and **interp'd Reality: Enjoy Your Stay**, named after [Karl Liebknecht](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karl_Liebknecht).

Karl is an average-sized human Rogue with black hair, fair skin, blue eyes and a handlebar moustache. He wears a grey fedora, a black flowing button-up suit over a white collar shirt and red tie, light grey pants, and dark brown business shoes. He is a relatively young character at 18 years old, yet in his past body before the **Critical Crash** he was much older at 35, almost twice his current age. Regardless of what form he is in, he has always been a budding revolutionary with a habit of smoking cigarettes, a natural-born leader of whatever adventuring party he winds up in.

Karl has the uncanny ability to mould his character in any situation to portray himself as an ally, despite his intentions being entirely neutral. He was originally a member of the **Bail Boys** clan before his capture by the **Xen People's Republic** and his clan's subsequent destruction. His prized possession was a [Steyr-Hahn M1912](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steyr_M1912_pistol) with the slide broken off, though it has since been replaced with the **Revolving Revolved Revolver**. He used to have the **Angeles TravelBuddy**, a supernatural tape recorder and player with dangerous properties, though he became aware of its danger and made several attempts to destroy it. He has also discovered a connection to **Colto**, one of the Harbringers, and has developed a relationship with [Bizzy](./bizzy).
