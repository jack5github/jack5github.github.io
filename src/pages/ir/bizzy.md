Bizzy (real name *Elizabeth Layton*) is **SweetCookie500**'s player character in the **interp'd Reality** campaign and **interp'd Reality: Enjoy Your Stay**.

Bizzy was a black cat person with a Druid build, whom was often barefoot while wearing a leafy green top with shoulders frills and slightly darker pants. She was a partial [tiefling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiefling) as a result of her radiation exposure in **Die Docks**, which caused her to change into a larger light-red-skinned humanoid with grey horns and near-black scleras, this transformation taking place roughly every 3 hours, determined by a random point on a linear scale. Since [episode 30 of **interp'd Reality**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9NOp51zm7ec), she is no longer affected by this and is permanently a tiefling.

Bizzy is an ex-**Breakniks** (by accident) and ex-gambler (by choice), who resided in **Die Docks** inside a tent, avoiding contact with the toxic sludge as best she could. One of her fixations was to put an end to **The Races**, which she used to be employed within when she would travel between **Die Docks** and **Trance Tundra** via **The Pipes**. She also wanted to return to the **Breakniks**, with or without her ex-friend **Ambient Generic**'s help, though she has since abandoned this desire of hers. In the **WasteNet**, she has a connection to the Harbringer **Lumun**, and has developed a relationship with [Karl](./karl).
