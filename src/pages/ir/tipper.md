Tipper is **Zeroman95**'s player character in the **interp'd Reality** campaign and **interp'd Reality: Enjoy Your Stay**. His design is heavily inspired by the characters from the video game [Stonehearth](https://store.steampowered.com/app/253250/Stonehearth/).

Tipper is a short medieval-era humanoid with a left-handed Fighter build, betrayed by his timid appearance. He wears a deep blue helmet which features a grey headband with bright blue wing-like protrusions on both sides, dark blue chain mail armour with grey plating, grey mittens, white arm bands and bulky brown boots. He is half the average height of most creatures, and his armour weighs him down, leading to him feeling small and needing assistance whenever he has to climb something. His weapon of choice is his "Great Sword", however, since it is back in **Gem Town** (his hometown), he has to make do with other non-magical swords instead.

Tipper is a very shy and reserved character who looks to others for guidance. Coming from a world further into the past than the one he has found himself in, and having done very little besides working on buildings and fighting goblin hordes, he is immensely confused by all modern terminology and depends on those more knowledgeable than him to make any decisions, especially [Karl](./karl). He also laments about **Gem Town**, concerned about the state of it and whether he'll ever find his way back to it again. Despite all of this, he tries his best to be a friendly guy, even if he needs to be carted around to expand his knowledge of modern subjects.
