Hmm (real name *Tahkaru*, pronounced *"tar-car-roo"*) is **Wolf-PvP**'s player character in the **interp'd Reality** campaign and **interp'd Reality: Enjoy Your Stay**.

Hmm is the bastard, gamer son of a noble family of large cold-blooded dragons from a far off kingdom. He himself is a not-as-big but still incredibly tall anthropomorphic dragon with a Wizard build, adorned in rugged red scales. He also has two small cream-coloured horns with tufts of black hair between them, dull green eyes and a mouth full of teeth, of which a single one protrudes out from his right side of his mouth when closed. His clothing consists of a dark red flowing robe with black sleeves and a darker-red scarf, plus a gold pendant and chain around his neck. Hmm's backpack is leather with a red and black design, multiple storage compartments and a small USA tassel on one of the zippers.

He is the youngest out of his party members (even after his age increase) and also the most immature, dealing with anger issues, violent tendencies and a general desire to be contrary to the status quo. He is also equipped with the most magic out of anyone in his adventuring party, with his spells mostly being of the attacking kind, as well as having an [unseen servant](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619209-unseen-servant) named **Alfred**. This has led to him becoming the black sheep of his party, one that does things those around him wouldn't even consider doing. Add to that his unnatural luck that has him uncovering useful items that are probably better off in others' hands, and you have a recipe for disaster.

He had a close albeit strained relationship with **Gary B. Gone**, whom he used to live across the street from and sometimes bully, before Gary was kicked out of the **WasteNet**. Since then, Hmm has become associated with **Floro**, who used to be Gary's Harbringer.
